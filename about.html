Our History

Mythic Minis was founded in 2025 with the goal of providing high-quality, affordable miniatures for tabletop gaming enthusiasts. Our team is passionate about gaming and dedicated to creating products that enhance the gaming experience.

We started as a small team of gamers and artists, and over the years, we've grown into a company that serves thousands of customers worldwide. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our success.
